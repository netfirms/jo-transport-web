About J.O Transportation and service Co..Ltd.

J.O Transportation and service Co..Ltd. is a leading car transport company with years of experience in the industry. We specialize in providing safe and reliable car transportation services for individuals and businesses.

Our team of professionals is dedicated to ensuring your vehicle reaches its destination in the same condition it was picked up. We take pride in our attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction.

With a modern fleet of transport vehicles and experienced drivers, we guarantee timely delivery and peace of mind for all our clients.

Our Commitment

We have to accept the fact that foreign businessmen and tourists often spend considerable amount of time sitting in limousine. Our staff have to create good impression on the clients' arrival, and provide efficient information and service relating to the services as require by clients. The main objective is to make them feel at home and appreciate with our services as well as to remember our country as a whole on their next journey. Our company are proud and appreciate the trust and confidence as well as cooperation and continuing support which we have granted from the service professionally.
